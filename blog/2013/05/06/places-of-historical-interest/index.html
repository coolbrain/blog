Places of Historical Interest

May 6th, 2013

If I have a dream, I should keep and protect it. Don’t let myself down. The outside world is beatiful, wonderful and unbelievable. Could I always keep hope in my heart? It’s easy to give up, to walk away; but it’s hard to stay, live and make things better. Don’t think about myself. Think about my parents, the people around me. Am I worthy?

2013.6月我去深圳的世界之窗，发现原来里面全是世界名胜古迹的赝品，在这里已经流连忘返、乐在其中，更何况是真正的名胜古迹。要知道自己的世界是由自己决定，是否成为井底之蛙，或者”会当凌绝顶，一览众山小”,就看自己的选择。只是仰慕、瞻仰是不够的，要有切实的行动。不单单是这样的事情，更多的事情也有现在的状态去决定。

I want to show some photos to inspire myself. Many great places are waiting me to see.

U.S Congress, the symbol of American democracy Washington Monument Lincoln Memorial. Lincoln is the most respectable president in America. History witnesses this great man. One hundred year later, Martin Luther King gave a great speech 《 I have a dream 》 at this place, 28 August 1963. Golden Gate Brige Canyonlands Utah. Natural scenary Maine Coast River Thames, London Millennium Bridge Buckingham Palace Standing gaurd, Ancient Egypt Temple of Horus Architectural marvel Christ the Redeemer, Brazil

人生有几多个十年？最紧要要过得痛快。其实很多的话都是meaningless，不会produce any effect，但好像我这样的人，就当作宝了。一个人不meditate，reflect，其实就不算人了，与动物无异。在历史面前，谦卑，景仰和沉思。

希望听起这首背景曲调时，会想起某些事，某些人，谁有能理解谁呢？时代已变，以前想都不敢想的观念，现在已经深入人心，但这个时代也有这时代的局限，让人觉得透不过起来，是啊，假如人不能适应现时代，那就–闷闷不乐，郁郁寡欢。金枝欲攀叹调.
