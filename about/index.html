Me

Mar 16th, 2013

I had ever lived in countryside where the river which I swam in was clean, the air was fresh and the people were kind.when I was a child who was required to do farm work, the people in the field called me nicely.When they worked for a long time and became tired,they would talk with me to eliminate tiring and let me have fun.

After the junior school, I had no need to help my family to do farm work except busy time. In high school,I focused myself on studying.After graduated from high school, I entered into South China University of Technology and majored in software engineering.

In college, I am confused, frustrated and paradoxical.As a freshman,I didn’t know what I need to do and where I should go.I couldn’t let myself calm down.It was hard for me to go through in that time.when I was sophomore, I began to make mistake.As a result,I suffered a lot as a junior and watched two series of TV which is BREAKOUT and LOST to console my broken heart.Now, I am a senior who is anxious to fix the hole and often watch rubbish series of TV .I am addicted to it because it can help me to forget the pain and feel numb.In short, I almost wasted all my time in college.I am not a qualified programmer because I didn’t spend much time in programming and improve myself. I felt guilty of my college life.

There is a saying:better late than never.Now, I am changing myself everyday, keeping learning and programming.

Maybe yesterday is not a good beginning, but today probably be.

从我从小到大的经历来看，我是一个无自制的人。我有今天的痛楚和失败，都是从昨天的不自制开始。饮食不自制，吃得过分饱，没有做到“食适可 勿过则”；食饭时匆匆忙忙、囫囵吞枣；养成饭后喝汤的坏习惯。做事情也是无自制。读书，要求间断地休息，自己不执行；坐姿，要求挺身直腰，自己不执行；看电视，要求只看一点，自己不执行，反而沉迷其中。我的不自制导致了我有肚腩，内脏器官出现障碍，从而脸色差；我的不自制导致我的近视一直在加深；我的不自制导致我在大学时居然得了面瘫；我的不自制导致我的大学一蹶不振。

曾经我也想去改变，但我根本没有意识到真正的问题所在。小学的时候，我对近视恐慌，不敢去测视力，是老师用暴力逼我去的。初中的时候，我对近视进行逃避。高中的时候，我开始想办法解决，还到中山大学附属医院进行检查，虽然没有得到什么明显的效果，起码我开始主动解决问题。大学的时候，对近视开始采取另外一种态度，想通过学习英语，减轻以后工作用眼的压力，可惜这次是郁郁而终。对我多年的大腿的患疾，我也开始积极面对，照X片，甚至大二的时候到广州市红十字会医院检查，虽然也没有得到什么效果。但毕竟我从高中开始就去解决问题。并且高中和大学都找过心理老师帮忙，高中对我有所帮助，但大学就没有效果了，也间接导致了我大学的颓废和失败。

回首过去，许多事情本来可以是好的，怎么都往坏的方向发展呢？要知道，假如自己是一个窝囊废，那么之后的路就绝路。要重新站起来，积极面对，主动解决问题，一个方法不行，采用另外一种方法，保持心情愉悦，脚踏实地。

I list the courses which I feel they are very useful and important.

- C/C++ language

- Data Structure

- Algorithm

- Compile Principle

- DataBase Principle Concept

- Operating System (linux,window)

- Computer Network

- Object Oriented Programming

- Software Architect

- Java language

- Mathamatics

- English
